Ingredients:
3 Scoops of Vanilla Ice Cream
1 Banana Split in Half
Chocolate Fudge
Whipped Cream
Cherry
Sprinkles

Howto:
-Place 3 scoops of Vanilla Ice Cream in a row on a long dish.
-Place halves of banana on both sides of ice cream scoops
-Top with Fudge, Whipped Cream and Sprinkles
-Add a cherry or 3.
